Welcome to GXLib Online Library

NIE Library is now a registered member of National Digital Library (NDL, An initiative of MHRD, Govt. of India). NDL provides support for all academic levels including researchers and life-long learners, all disciplines, all popular form of access devices and differently-abled learners. It is being developed to help students to prepare for entrance and competitive examination, to enable people to learn and prepare from best practices from all over the world and to facilitate researchers to perform inter-linked exploration from multiple sources. It is being developed at IIT Kharagpur.
